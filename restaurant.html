Our Story
A table worth coming back to.

The Harvest Table opened in 2019 with one simple idea: build a menu around what Iowa farmers are actually growing right now, not what a supply chain can ship cheaply.

Chef Mara Lindqvist sources from eleven local farms within sixty miles of Des Moines. The menu is rewritten every Tuesday. The wine list changes every month.

We seat 48 guests, take reservations for parties of two or more, and host private events on Sunday evenings.
