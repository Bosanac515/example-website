What We Do
Our Programs

From mobile food pantries to after-school tutoring, every program targets a real, documented need in our community.

Food Access

Roots Food Pantry

Weekly mobile distributions at 6 locations across Des Moines. No paperwork, no income verification — just groceries for families who need them.

Learn more →
Youth

After-School Academy

Free tutoring, mentorship, and enrichment programs for K–8 students at four Des Moines Public Schools locations, Monday through Thursday.

Learn more →
Community

Neighbor Connect

Monthly community events, a resource referral hotline, and one-on-one case navigation to connect families with housing, utilities, and health services.

Learn more →
